Four Geocoders Comparisons

  • Comparison of four Geopy geocoders: September 2018 vs. April 2019 results

    Part I: Quantitative summary: pairwise differences as distances

    Overall, the results from AzureMaps differ the most from the others, especially for Kings (Brooklyn) and Richmond (Staten Island) counties, as is shown by their respective maps: Slides X, and Y.

    Notes:
    I gave a generous 5 km (~3.1 miles) allowance for heatmaping the distance differences; this means that the color scale applies to distances that are greater than that.
    I did not find the NYC equivalent 'shapefile with waters' for Boston.

    Part II: Maps!

    In my updated implementation, I figured out how to draw the box from the shapefiles. The map elements from the shapefile are color-coded black in the legends; these are called "Boro bounds" in the old maps, "shapefile bounds" and "shapefile box" in the new ones. The color of elements for each geocoder is also slightly changed (desaturated).

    Finally, the embedded Folium maps are fully functional:   The zoom indication in the captions helps visualize out-of-bounds results; The map elements, which overlap if identical, can be toggled on/off in the legend.
